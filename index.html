About Me

With 6 years of experience in interactive art, I specialize in designing and delivering immersive VR solutions that merge digital and physical environments. My expertise spans human-computer interaction, 3D modeling, UI/UX design, and sensor-based installations. I help organizations, festivals, and creative teams translate complex concepts into engaging, high-impact experiences. Having collaborated with international events like Ars Electronica and managed VR technology for large-scale productions, I bring a blend of artistic vision and technical precision to projects that aim to inspire, educate, and connect audiences.
