Live The Moment

VR / Kinetic Installation

  • Co-creator:
    Sin-Ying Bai / Yan-Sin Lin
  • Theme:
    Speculation about the future, the connection between virtual and real space
  • Year:
    May 2022
  • Media:
    Animation, Mobile Platforms, Composite Media
  • Size:
    2 × 6 × 3 (m)

Statement

We made a specific emergency about future anxiety, and made them "happen" in the exhibition hall. Using the anticipatory psychology that people will have when viewing the virtual world, we created a completely different environment before and after viewing, as if suddenly incidents do happen in the real world.

Production Process

  • Conceive and design
  • 3D modeling
  • 3D Scene construction
  • Mobile platform motor programming
  • Animation design
  • Real scene arrangement
  • Mobile platform assembly, VR device setup
