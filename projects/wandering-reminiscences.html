Wandering Reminiscences

Video, Interactive Installation

  • Co-creator:
    Kai-Jui Cheng
  • Theme:
    Time and memory
  • Year:
    April 2021
  • Media:
    Video, Wood, Metal, Sensor
  • Size:
    2 x 4 x 4 (m)

Statement

When the present becomes the past, what has happened becomes the memory and farther and farther as time passes. However, when we open the box of memories in our minds, the gradually dimming picture can reappear, replay, and finally fade again, constantly reincarnating at the moment we remember. The work looks like a wall of clocks for a distance. Once approached, videos will appear on the clock surface one by one. In the strong light projection, the hands will gradually be submerged, replaced by the daily scenery that appears. Finally, when the audience leaves, they will return to the normal clock wall and continue to record the current time.

Production Process

  • Interactive Programming (Ultrasonic Sensor, Arduino, Processing)
  • Image projection output (Obs Studio)
  • Space modeling design and woodwork
  • Video recording (Phone for photography and editing)
