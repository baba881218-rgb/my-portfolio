Out of the Bound

360 VR videos and Live streaming

  • Theme:
    Time and space in out-of-body experiences
  • Year:
    May 2024
  • Media:
    360 Video, 360 Camera
  • Size:
    3 × 6 × 3 (m)

Statement

Through virtual reality experiences, the illusion of separation between the soul and the body is created. When future technology enables consciousness to exist independently of the body, will people still perceive time and space in the same way? Or perhaps our perception of time and space will be "filtered" by technology?

Production Process

  • Automatic moving camera tripod (motor, track, pulley, tripod)
  • 360 video (Insta360 Pro, Premiere)
  • 360 live stream (Insta360 Pro, Obs Studio)
  • Project operation (Unreal Engine 5)

Interactive Process

  1. The audience puts on the VR headset.
  2. A pre-recorded panoramic video taken at the exhibition is played in VR, with the viewpoint automatically moving forward until it stops at the end of the track, while the audience remains standing in place.
  3. The video switches to a live stream from the 360 camera placed at the end of the track, allowing the audience to turn around and see themselves.
  4. The view switches back to the pre-recorded video, with the viewpoint moving backward to the audience's standing position.

Exhibition in Rondo Sztuki, Katowice, Poland
